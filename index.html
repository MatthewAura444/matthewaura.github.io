ABOUT

At 16, I immerse myself in technology through functional code, working with C++, C#, Python, Java, and JavaScript. My approach is straightforward — code should be efficient, accessible, and solve real problems. I dedicate significant time to optimizing performance and ensuring clean architecture. Rather than getting lost in theoretical complexities, I focus on developing solutions with tangible real-world applications. Each project represents an opportunity to expand my technical boundaries, experiment with emerging technologies, and implement industry best practices that ensure high-quality execution. This practical mindset helps me create software that not only works well but also provides genuine value to users.

I've developed a strong interest in cryptography and actively pursue its study, having already implemented advanced encryption systems in several projects. My work has ranged from symmetric key algorithms and hash functions to exploring zero-knowledge proofs and blockchain security models. I've built secure communication channels, authentication systems, and data protection mechanisms that withstand modern attack vectors. Rather than simply following trends, I approach these security systems with methodical analysis — deconstructing their mathematical foundations, studying their vulnerabilities, and applying them to create robust, tamper-resistant applications that solve actual security challenges. This passion for cryptography has led me to participate in several CTF competitions, where I've honed my skills in both offensive and defensive security techniques.

Reading serves as my foundation for knowledge and a deeper understanding of the world. Books provide insights that shape my perspectives and expand my thinking beyond conventional boundaries. I maintain a disciplined reading schedule, covering diverse genres from technical manuals to philosophical works, ensuring continuous intellectual growth. I especially value technical literature that enhances my programming skills and introduces innovative concepts that can revolutionize software development. The analytical thinking required for comprehending complex texts parallels the logical approach needed in development work, creating a synergy between my reading habits and coding practices. Books on systems thinking and design patterns have particularly influenced my architectural approaches. Beyond technical knowledge, literature on psychology and human behavior helps me design more intuitive user interfaces and experiences. Books aren't just sources of information – they inspire creative solutions and methodological approaches that I integrate into my coding projects, resulting in more elegant and effective software systems.

A fundamental text for both philosophical and religious Taoism, it also strongly influenced other schools of Chinese philosophy and religion, including Legalism, Confucianism, and Buddhism, which was largely interpreted through the use of Taoist words and concepts.
